# ShareMyRun

ShareMyRun is an iOS app for generating workout share images from your run data, customizing what appears on those images, and quickly sharing results.

## Features & Benefits

- Create shareable workout images from recent runs.
- Choose which stats are shown so the image reflects what matters most to you.
- Mark a featured stat to emphasize one key result.
- Customize visual style (font, size, text position, text color, and output format).
- Select background styles (route map, recent photo, or a chosen photo).
- Share directly or save to Photos for later posting.
- Auto-share workflows help reduce post-run friction when sharing with accountability groups.

## Why I Built This

I wanted a way to customize the stats displayed on the images generated by the Apple Fitness app, and to automatically share those with my accountability group chat after a run.

## Experimental Status

This is an experimental app, and most of the codebase was generated with AI tools. Use it at your own risk.
